Below is a list of my projects I have worked on in the past. Do note that some are still under construction as I am yet to write them up.

Computational Fluid Dynamics

Computational Fluid Dynamics is an excellent tool to understand fluid and heat flow. The equations describing these flows are often highly convoluted. Thus, numerical analysis is required to approximate these equations.

Heat Transfer Augmentation from Extended Surfaces using Dimples

Heat transfer is one of the most important processes in the electronics industry. In this project, I devised a way to increase heat transfer from extended surfaces, often called heat fins, by adding dimples to their surfaces.

Deep Learning

Having developed a deep interest in computational fluid dynamics, AI and machine learning was the next frontier I wanted to capture. Some wonder why a fluid dynamist wants to learn about AI, but there is a very unique overlap between the two fields. I am trying to explore this overlap through understanding basics of machine learning and taking that forward to solving fluid dynamics problems.
